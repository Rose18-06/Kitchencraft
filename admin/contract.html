Catering Service Agreement Form

This Agreement is made between Loreto's Kitchenette ("the Caterer") and the Customer ("the Client"). By signing this Agreement, the Client agrees to the following terms and conditions:

Charges Outside Venue

  1. Event Extension: If the event extends beyond the agreed duration, an additional charge of Php 1,000 per hour will apply.
  2. Menu Changes After Reservation:
    • The Client may request changes to the menu 2-5 days before the reservation date, provided that the preparation of the menu has not yet commenced.
    • Any menu changes requested after the specified timeframe or once preparation has begun will incur additional charges, which will depend on the complexity and cost of the new menu.
    • Additional charges may apply if the requested changes result in costs exceeding the original menu price.

Salary of Staff (Overtime)

  1. The usual duration of an event is 3-4 hours.
  2. If the event exceeds this duration, a corresponding overtime charge will apply, and the staff's salary will be doubled for the extended hours.

Policy on Cancellation and Incidents

  1. In the event of a cancellation by the Client, 20% of the down payment will be forfeited.
  2. This forfeiture also applies to other unforeseen incidents that may prevent the event from proceeding as planned.

Damages to Property

  1. The Client is responsible for any damage to items or property owned by Loreto's Kitchenette during the event.
  2. Examples include, but are not limited to, broken plates, chairs, or other equipment. The Client will be charged the cost of repair or replacement for any damaged items

Acknowledgment and Agreement

By signing below, the Client acknowledges that they have read, understood, and agree to the terms and conditions outlined in this Agreement.
